Question: Which customers have placed the most orders?
```cypher
MATCH (c:Customer)-[:PLACED]->(s:SalesOrder)
RETURN c.fullName AS customer, COUNT(s) AS orderCount
ORDER BY orderCount DESC
LIMIT 10
```

Question: Find sales orders with no billing documents

Question: Which products appear in the most sales orders?
```cypher
MATCH (p:Product)<-[:CONTAINS]-(s:SalesOrder)
```

Question: Show all deliveries shipped from a specific plant
```cypher
MATCH (d:Delivery)-[:SHIPS_FROM]->(pl:Plant)

LIMIT 50
```

Question: What is the total billing amount per customer?
```cypher
MATCH (c:Customer)-[:PLACED]->(s:SalesOrder)-[:HAS_BILLING]->(b:BillingDocument)

LIMIT 50
```
